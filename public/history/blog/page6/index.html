Project Euler030 with python

pignu - October 29, 2017

Surprisingly there are only three numbers that can be written as the sum of fourth powers of their digits: \[\begin{align} 1634 = 1^4 + 6^4 + 3^4 + 4^4 \\ 8208 = 8^4 + 2^4 + 0^4 + 8^4 \\ 9474 = 9^4+4^4+7^4+4^4 \\ \end{align}\] As 1 = 14 is not a sum it is not included. The sum of these numbers is 1634 + 8208 + 9474 = 19316. Find the sum of all the numbers that can be written as the sum of fifth powers of their digits.
